			Data Dictionary for Weather Dataset
				DM Essentials I Project


Date
The date of observation.

Location
The common name of the location of the weather station.

MinTemp
The minimum temperature in degrees Celsius.

MaxTemp
The maximum temperature in degrees Celsius.

Rainfall
The amount of rainfall recorded for the day in mm.

Evaporation
Evaporation (mm) in the 24 hours to 9am.

Sunshine
The number of hours of bright sunshine in the day.

WindGustDir
The direction of the strongest wind gust in the 24 hours to midnight.

WindGustSpeed
The speed (km/hr) of the strongest wind gust in the 24 hours to midnight.

WindDir9am
Wind direction averaged over 10 minutes prior to 9am.

WindDir3pm
Wind direction averaged over 10 minutes prior to 3pm.

WindSpeed9am
Wind speed (km/hr) averaged over 10 minutes prior to 9am.

WindSpeed3pm
Wind speed (km/hr) averaged over 10 minutes prior to 3pm.

Humidity9am
Relative humidity (percent) at 9am.

Humidity3pm
Relative humidity (percent) at 3pm.

Pressure9am
Atmospheric pressure (hectopascals) at 9am, reduced to mean sea level.

Pressure3pm
Atmospheric pressure (hectopascals) at 3pm, reduced to mean sea level.

Cloud9am
Fraction of sky obscured by cloud at 9am. This is measured in "oktas", which are a unit of eighths. It records how many eighths of the sky are obscured by cloud. A 0 measure indicates completely clear sky whilst an 8 indicates that it is completely overcast.

Cloud3pm
Fraction of sky obscured by cloud at 3pm. See Cloud9am for description.

Temp9am
Temperature (degrees Celsius) at 9am.

Temp3pm
Temperature (degrees Celsius) at 3pm.

RainToday
1 if precipitation (mm) recorded for the day exceeds 1mm; otherwise 0.

RainTomorrow
1 if precipitation (mm) recorded for the NEXT day exceeds 1m; otherwise 0.
